About SkillSwap

Connecting With Students Through Skill Exchange

SkillSwap is a vibrant online platform designed to bring together students from Campus to exchange their skills and knowledge. Whether you're passionate about web development, graphic design, or language learning, SkillSwap provides a dynamic environment where students can connect, collaborate, and learn from each other.

Our mission is to create a community where students can harness their talents, expand their horizons, and build meaningful connections with like-minded individuals. With a diverse range of categories and an intuitive interface, SkillSwap makes it easy for students to find the help they need and share their expertise with others.

Frequently Asked Questions

Explore answers to common questions about our platform.

To join SkillSwap as a student, simply sign up using your email address and create your profile. Make sure to verify your email to access all features available exclusively to students.

As a student, you can browse available projects by logging into your SkillSwap account and navigating to the Projects section. Filter projects by category or search for specific keywords to find projects that match your skills and interests.

SkillSwap offers various payment options for students, including bank transfer and PayPal. Once you complete a project, the client will release payment according to the agreed terms. Make sure to update your payment preferences in your account settings to receive payments seamlessly.

If you encounter any issues with a project or client on SkillSwap, you can report them to our support team. Simply navigate to the Help Center and submit a ticket detailing the problem you're experiencing. Our team will investigate the issue and provide assistance to resolve it promptly.
